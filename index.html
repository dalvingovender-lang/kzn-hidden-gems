About KZN Hidden Gems

A community-driven platform built to help KwaZulu-Natal residents explore the outdoors safely and confidently.

🌿 Our Mission

KZN Hidden Gems was built to solve a critical problem in South African outdoor recreation: the lack of a trusted, safety-first platform dedicated to KwaZulu-Natal's diverse natural spaces.

Global platforms like AllTrails and iOverlander lack the hyper-local granularity needed to navigate KZN's complex mix of community reserves, provincial parks, and privately managed nature areas — each with unique security conditions, payment requirements, and environmental hazards.

Our platform addresses this gap by providing a weighted Desirability and Safety Index (D_i) that prioritizes personal security and parking safety above all other factors — because in KZN, safety is the ultimate gatekeeper for outdoor recreation.

📐 The Desirability Index Formula

D_i = (0.35 × Safety) + (0.25 × Parking) + (0.15 × Environment) + (0.15 × Cleanliness) − (0.10 × Cost)

Safety (35%) and Parking Security (25%) together account for 60% of a location's overall score. A stunning location with poor personal security or unsecured trailhead parking will score significantly lower, preventing users from stumbling into risky situations. This formula is calibrated specifically for the Durban urban-wilderness interface.
